New `power: 'lean' | 'god'` config governs Pantheon orchestration ceremony in AI-assistant adapter output. `lean` (default) routes to one specialist with a one-line Zeus header and no auto-council; `god` unlocks the full multi-line routing banners, council assembly/report blocks, and deep-research escalation. `thesmos advise` now assigns a model recommendation per plan phase (splitting a plan across model tiers when phases genuinely differ in depth) instead of a single recommendation for the whole plan.
