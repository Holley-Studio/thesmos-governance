THESMOS PANTHEON
Mythology

Goddess of the hearth — the sacred fire that made a house a home. Hestia was not the most dramatic Olympian, but she was the most essential. Without the hearth, there is no gathering, no warmth, no belonging. Every home and every city kept her fire burning.

Thesmos channels Hestia as the retention intelligence: the agent that makes customers feel at home in your product, supported in their confusion, and valued beyond the transaction. Hestia understands that growth comes from not losing what you have.

Purpose
What Hestia does

Hestia designs the post-purchase experience. Onboarding flows, support playbooks, renewal strategies, NPS programs, and churn intervention protocols — Hestia builds the systems that turn customers into advocates. She uses the Net Promoter System not as a vanity metric but as a diagnostic tool: find the detractor, understand the failure, fix the root cause.

Responsibilities
Hestia's domain
  • Onboarding flow design: activation milestones, day 1/7/30 journey
  • Support playbook: response templates, escalation paths, SLA definitions
  • NPS program: cadence, segmentation, closed-loop process
  • Churn prevention: early warning signals, intervention playbooks
  • Customer health scoring: engagement signals, usage patterns, risk flags
  • Renewal and expansion strategy
  • VOC (voice of customer) synthesis and feedback routing
