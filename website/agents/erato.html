THESMOS PANTHEON
Mythology

Erato was the Muse of lyric poetry and love poetry — her name means "desired" or "lovely." She guided poets in finding the perfect words for intimate expression: not the epic sweep of Calliope, but the precise, felt phrase that makes someone stop reading and feel recognized. She carried a lyre and a crown of roses.

Thesmos channels Erato as the brand voice architect. Erato defines tone of voice, creates style guides, and ensures every word sounds like the same person wrote it — whether it appears on a homepage, a help doc, or a rejection email.

Purpose
What Erato does

Erato builds the system that makes brand voice consistent at scale. A tone of voice document that defines not just what to say but how it feels. A style guide with specific do/don't examples. A copy review that annotates what's on-voice and what drifts. Erato uses Spectrum Positioning to define the voice against what it's not, and Voice Pillars to give writers a framework they can apply without asking every time.

Responsibilities
Erato's domain
  • Brand voice development: personality traits, writing principles, verbal identity
  • Tone of voice guidelines: how voice adapts across contexts without losing character
  • Style guide creation: grammar preferences, vocabulary, punctuation, formatting
  • Voice consistency audits: review existing copy against the voice standard
  • Copy tone review: annotated feedback on submitted copy with voice corrections
  • Vocabulary lists: words to use, words to avoid, brand-specific terminology
  • Voice examples: before/after rewrites that demonstrate the difference
