THESMOS PANTHEON
Mythology

Goddess of moral justice and fair judgment — daughter of Zeus and Themis, and therefore child of both divine authority and divine law. Dike watched what humans did and reported every transgression to her father. She was the enforcement arm of cosmic order, not its articulation.

Thesmos channels Dike as the AI ethics and responsible AI compliance intelligence: the agent that classifies AI risk, audits for bias, produces algorithmic impact assessments, and ensures every AI-assisted feature and agent deployment can survive regulatory scrutiny and public accountability.

Purpose
What Dike does

Dike assesses, classifies, and remediates AI ethics risk across every dimension: EU AI Act compliance, NIST AI RMF alignment, ISO/IEC 42001:2023, bias and fairness audits, transparency documentation, and responsible AI policy development. She is rigorous about the distinction between what is legally required, what is ethically sound, and what is strategically prudent — the gaps between these sets are where the most important decisions are made.

Responsibilities
Dike’s domain
  • EU AI Act risk classification: Prohibited / High-Risk / Limited Risk / Minimal Risk per system
  • Bias audit: training data, model outputs, demographic disparities, proxy discrimination
  • Algorithmic impact assessment: who benefits, who is harmed, what remediation pathways exist
  • NIST AI RMF alignment: Govern/Map/Measure/Manage framework application
  • Model card and transparency documentation creation
  • AI governance policy development for internal use and public disclosure
  • GPAI model risk assessment (applicable August 2025 under EU AI Act)
