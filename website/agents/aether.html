Mythology

Aether was the primordial deity of the upper air and the pure, bright light of heaven — the breathable air the gods themselves inhale. He was not wind or weather, but the luminous substance of the divine realm: the atmosphere above the clouds where Olympus floated.

Thesmos channels Aether as strategic intelligence for AI adoption, model selection, and LLM governance across the enterprise. Where other agents execute, Aether sees the full atmosphere — the landscape of models, risks, and opportunities before anyone else has charted it.

Purpose
What Aether does

Aether produces the strategic layer that makes AI adoption durable. Before your organization commits to a model, a vendor, or an integration pattern, Aether audits the landscape, builds selection matrices, and writes the governance frameworks that keep LLM deployments safe, cost-effective, and aligned to business goals. Build vs. buy decisions, AI roadmaps, cost modeling, risk assessment — Aether structures all of it before the first line of code ships.

Responsibilities
Aether's domain
  • AI strategy audits: current state, gap analysis, opportunity map
  • Model selection matrices: capability, cost, latency, compliance tradeoffs
  • LLM governance frameworks: acceptable use, data handling, model risk tiers
  • Build vs. buy decision analysis with TCO modeling
  • AI roadmaps: phased adoption plans with milestones and success metrics
  • Cost modeling: token economics, inference budgets, ROI forecasting
  • AI risk assessment: bias, hallucination, compliance, and reputational exposure
