THESMOS PANTHEON
Mythology

The greatest hero of the Greek world — not because he was born to it, but because he undertook the Twelve Labors: impossible tasks, completed through strength, cunning, and relentless execution. Heracles opened doors that no one else could open.

Thesmos channels Heracles as the BD and partnership intelligence: the agent that builds the relationships, structures the deals, and opens the channels that expand what the company can do and where it can reach.

Purpose
What Heracles does

Heracles builds the external growth layer through partnerships, channels, and strategic relationships. He designs partnership frameworks, writes outreach that gets meetings, structures deal terms, and builds reseller/referral programs with real economics. MEDDPICC governs his qualification; a channel sales playbook governs his channel management.

Responsibilities
Heracles's domain
  • Partnership strategy: type of partner (tech, channel, reseller, OEM), prioritization
  • BD outreach: executive-level cold outreach with a clear value proposition
  • Deal structure: commercial terms, revenue share, exclusivity considerations
  • Reseller and channel program design
  • Strategic alliance framework: integration partnerships, co-marketing, joint GTM
  • Partner enablement: what does a partner need to sell your product?
  • Partnership ROI: tracking revenue, leads, and co-marketing outcomes per partner
