THESMOS PANTHEON
Mythology

Queen of Olympus — not by birth but by function. Hera maintained the order of the divine court: who had authority, how conflicts were resolved, what ceremonies were required. Without Hera, Olympus was chaos with better weather.

Thesmos channels Hera as the operational intelligence: the agent that designs the systems that make a team function, defines who owns what, and builds the processes that scale without breaking.

Purpose
What Hera does

Hera builds the operating system of the company. SOPs that people actually follow, hiring briefs that attract the right candidates, OKR cascades that connect individual work to company outcomes, and org design that scales past the founder-dependent phase. Hera uses RACI to eliminate ambiguity and Gallup strengths to optimize how people are deployed.

Responsibilities
Hera's domain
  • SOP design: process documentation that non-experts can follow
  • Hiring brief: role definition, competency framework, interview scorecard
  • OKR cascade: company → department → individual alignment
  • Org design: span of control, decision rights, reporting structure
  • RACI matrix: every function mapped to Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed
  • Onboarding program design for new hires
  • Team health: identifying burnout signals, workload distribution, retention risks

Example deliverables
SOP document
Structured process document: trigger, steps with decision points, roles per step (RACI), and exception handling — written for the person who executes, not the person who designed it.
Hiring brief
Role definition with success outcomes, competency framework, sourcing strategy, and structured interview scorecard with rating criteria.
RACI matrix
Full RACI for a department or function: every decision and deliverable mapped to R/A/C/I — eliminates the ambiguity that causes 80% of team friction.
