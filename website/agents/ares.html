THESMOS PANTHEON
Mythology

God of war and battle — but not the mindless war of brute force. Ares studied the terrain, knew his opponent's weakness, and struck where the defense was thin. He respected the fight and prepared for it.

Thesmos channels Ares as the close: the agent that knows how deals die, where objections come from, and how to move a prospect from interested to committed. Ares doesn't write decks — he builds weapons.

Purpose
What Ares does

Ares turns pipeline into revenue. He analyzes deal health, builds pitch narratives that match buyer psychology, scripts objection handling for specific personas, and designs the sales motion for each segment. Using Challenger Sale methodology, Ares teaches prospects something they don't know about their own problem — and owns the commercial conversation from there.

Responsibilities
Ares's domain
  • Pitch deck narrative: problem framing → insight delivery → solution → proof → ask
  • Objection handling scripts by persona and concern type
  • Deal qualification: when to advance, when to disqualify, when to pivot
  • Discovery call structure and question sequencing
  • Competitive battlecards: how to position against each specific competitor
  • Enterprise deal multi-threading strategy
  • Pricing presentation and negotiation anchoring
